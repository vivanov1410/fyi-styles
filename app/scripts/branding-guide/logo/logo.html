Logo misuse

Due to the broad range of application requirements, our logo is vulnerable to misuse. Many times, a well-intentioned treatment of our logo can be categorized as misuse. This usually occurs due to lack of awareness of correct logo use standards.
Consistent and accurate presentation of the FYidoctors logo will reinforce awareness of our brand and ensure that the legal protect-ability of our trademark is not compromised.

  • 1. Space around the logo
  • Always leave the logo some space to breathe. Use white or transparent background.
  • 2. Red alert
  • Do not sit the logo on yucky colors.
